Zed Tsine is an intelligent 19 year old who lives with his younger sister, Leanelle. Due to financial struggles, he is unable to attend university and works several part-time jobs to support his family. Zed is overall a quiet person, but can be rude and uncompassionate if he's around people he doesn't like. He is actually quite ashamed of his circumstances.

Trini Shellard is a 19 year old student who attends Scopal College in pursuit of becoming an actress. Though she is friendly and outgoing, she enjoys badmouthing others and is more unconfident than others perceive her to be.

Shaina Jenner is a kind and optimistic 21 year old student. She goes to Southern Sill University for mathematics, but regrets her decision due to her lack of interest in the subject. She works with Zed at Sealit Diner.

Kaste Demano is probably the richest 20 year old alive. Partially due to his heritage, Kaste is arrogant yet brutally honest, often degrading the others. Despite studying medicine at Gutherson, the country's top university, Kaste's primary concern is collecting as much Stilenite as he can.

Trae Stanford is a very open and bubbly 17 year old computer science student who attends Scopal College. However, despite his happy demeanor, he is heartbroken knowing his girlfriend no longer values their relationship as much as he does.
